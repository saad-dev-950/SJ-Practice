Rita Cetina Universal

Basic Education Scholarship

For students under the age of 18 enrolled in public secondary schools in Mexico.

Support granted

Every two months starting in September 2025:

  • $1,900 MXN per family with at least one student in public secondary school.
  • $700 MXN additional for each extra student in public secondary school.

Studying is a right

Learn how to register your children or dependents

Requirements:

From the mother, father or guardian:

  1. CURP (Check it here )
  2. Cell phone number
  3. Email
  4. Digitized official identification
  5. Digital proof of address (dated no more than three months prior)

From the student(s) to be registered:

  1. CURP (Check it here )
  2. Work Center Code (Check it here )

Steps for registration:

  1. Log in or create an account on Llave MX .
  2. Register your information as a mother, father or guardian.
  3. Register each of the students.
  4. Done! The information is now in the validation stage to continue the process.

Important: If your children or dependents are enrolled in a public high school during the 2025-2026 school year, register them! If they already have the scholarship, it is not necessary to register them again.

Did you know?

Rita Cetina was one of the first Mexican women to promote women's education.

Today, his legacy lives on through a scholarship that bears his name, supporting students committed to social transformation.
