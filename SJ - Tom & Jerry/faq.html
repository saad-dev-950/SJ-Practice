What is SJ?

SJ is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies on an internet-connected device.

Depending on your plan, you can also download TV shows and movies to your Android phone or tablet, iPhone, iPad, or Google Chromebook device and watch without an internet connection.

If you're already a member and would like to learn more about using SJ, visit Getting started with SJ.

TV Shows & Movies

SJ content varies by region and may change over time. You can watch a variety of award-winning SJ originals, TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more.

The more you watch, the better SJ gets at recommending TV shows and movies.

Supported Devices

You can watch SJ through any supported internet-connected device that offers the SJ app, including smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, cable boxes, smartphones, and tablets. You can also watch SJ on your computer using an internet browser. To get the best performance, you can review the system requirements for web browser compatibility and check our internet speed recommendations.

NOTE: A small percentage of devices may not be supported by all plans.

Need help getting set up? Search our Help Center for the manufacturer of the device you're using.

NOTE: The SJ app may come pre-loaded on certain devices, or you may need to download the SJ app onto your device. SJ app functionality may differ between devices.

Plans and Pricing

Each SJ plan determines the number of devices you can watch SJ on at the same time and whether you can watch in High Definition (HD), Full High Definition (FHD), or Ultra High Definition (UHD).

You can change your plan or cancel online at any time.

Get Started

To start watching SJ:

  1. Visit SJ.com/signup.
  2. Choose a plan.
  3. Create an account by entering your email address and creating a password.
  4. Enter a payment method.

As a SJ member, you are charged once a month on the date you signed up.

General Description

This cookie tool will help you understand the use of cookies on the SJ service, and how you can control the use of these cookies.

Privacy settings in most browsers allow you to prevent your browser from accepting some or all cookies, notify you when it receives a new cookie, or disable cookies altogether. If your browser disables all cookies, then information will not be collected or stored via the cookies listed in this tool. This means that your use of the SJ service may be impaired.

Please note that when you use this cookie tool to opt out of certain cookies, your opt out preferences are recorded by placing a cookie on your device. Therefore, your browser must be configured to accept cookies in order for your preferences to take effect. Also, if you delete or clear your cookies, or change your web browser, you will need to reset your cookie preferences.

For more information on our use of cookies, please visit the Cookies and Internet Advertising section of our Privacy Statement.
